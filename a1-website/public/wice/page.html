The Lich King Kill

Seven years ago, i started to play a game called world of warcraft on a private server that was called Warmane. On this private server, i always wanted to kill the last boss in the game called The Lich King. So I did start by level my character from level 0 to level 80. Then when i was level 80 i started to find a group of people that wanted to kill the hardest boss together with me. For this to work, we needed 25 players. So it took some time to gather all those players, but once we had all those players, we started to get better gear for our characters to be able to kill all the bosses leading up to the most difficult boss in the game. To kill all the bosses leading up to the most difficult boss in the game took us around 6 months, but then finally after all that time we did reach the last boss and were able to kill him in just a few weeks.

The image I have in this text shows that The Lich King (The final boss of the game) is defeated and it also shows most of the players that were there to help me with this goal. Some of these players still play on that server to this day, and others moved on to later version of the game to fight even more difficult challanges that the world of warcraft has to offer.

By Wictor Rindebrant,
Student at Linneuniversitetet.
